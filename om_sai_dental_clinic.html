Dr. Kirankumar Hasbe

With over 15 years of dedicated practice in prosthodontics and implantology, Dr. Kirankumar Hasbe is one of Pune's most trusted dental specialists. Having trained at AFMC Pune and completed fellowship programs in the USA, he brings world-class expertise to every patient's smile journey.
